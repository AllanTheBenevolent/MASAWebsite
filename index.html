One Family One Heartbeat

Focused on fostering the relationship between one another, we want to create a place that you can call a home away from home. We're more than just an organization, we are a family. Check out the video for a first person view of how its like to be part of MASA.


FREE FOOD
Our Greatest Asset.

You read it right! Three words comes into mind when you hear the word MASA, Friends, Family, and FOOD. Yes, food! We understand the financial expenses and turmoil many students face when going to college. Our solution is to provide free food events for our members. Who doesn't like eating great food while making new friends?!
