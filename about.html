ABOUT US

The Multi-Asian Student Association (MASA) is an all-inclusive sociocultural organization at the University of California at San Diego that aims to celebrate and promote all Asian cultures. Since 1999, MASA has provided a place for people to make new friends in a college atmosphere. Together, we are "one family, one heartbeat".





THE TRINITY

To best support its values, MASA formed two affiliated organizations—kunFusion and FUSION. kunFusion is MASA’s all-inclusive dance team and it provides an environment where individuals can gather and physically express themselves. As for other means of growth, FUSION is MASA’s fully student run dance competition, which has grown to be one of the largest of the west coast due to the dedication and professionalism of its staff.
